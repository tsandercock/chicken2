Fox in the Hen House Our definition: Someone who is in a place where he/she does not belong and will likely create havoc. Origin: A-Z Animals in its blog references back to 1589 notation in “The Contre- League and Answer to Certain Letters Sent to the Maisters of Renes by One of the League and Gentleman of the Late Duke of Guizes Traine.
